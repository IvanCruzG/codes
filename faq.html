Frenquently Asked Questions

Can I borrow a phone charger at library?

Yes, library locations have various options for charging phones. The TFDL has a charger station with 8 lockable charging units. It is located next to the 1st floor Information Desk.

Does the library have software: ArcGIS, SPSS, Stata, SAS, NVivo?

Spatial and Numeric Data Services, Taylor Family Digital Library 4th floor, has GIS and statistical software: ArcGIS, SPSS, Stata, SAS and NVivo. Please contact SANDS for more information.

How many pages can I copy from a book without breaking copyright?

Fair Dealing Guidlines are a provision fo the Copyright Act that allows for some copying of materials without getting permission from the copyright owner or paying a copyright royalty. Check out the Guidelines from our Copyright Page- Fair Dealing tab.
